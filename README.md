# dollar-index-quant-visual-lab
The Dollar Universe: Terrain Mapping &amp; Volatility Galaxy of the US Dollar Index

This dataset contains daily historical data for the US Dollar Index (DXY) starting from the year 2000.

Fields Included:

Date — Trading date

Open — Opening price

High — Highest price

Low — Lowest price

Close — Closing price

Volume — Trading volume

Coverage:
2000 – Present

Potential Research Applications:

• Foreign exchange market analysis

• Volatility regime detection

• Quantitative macro research

• Financial time-series modeling

• Currency risk analysis

• Data visualization experiments
